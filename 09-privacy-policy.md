# Privacy Policy Draft

_Last updated: April 3, 2026_

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the platform collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information when users access the website or app, create accounts, submit reviews, claim profiles, or interact with public listings.

This draft should be reviewed by legal counsel before production use.

## 2. What This Platform Does

The platform hosts public-facing profiles and user-submitted reviews about entities such as landlords, rentals, businesses, clinics, professionals, and service providers.

Some profiles may be created by users before the profiled party has an account.

## 3. Information We Collect

## 3.1 Information you provide
- phone number
- profile name or username
- reviews and ratings
- reports and appeals
- claim request information
- support messages
- optional profile details you choose to add

## 3.2 Information collected automatically
- IP address
- device/browser metadata
- timestamps
- usage analytics
- security logs
- cookies/session identifiers

## 3.3 Information about entities
The platform may store business or entity information submitted by users, such as:
- name
- category
- city/area
- address or landmark
- branch or property identifiers
- optional public phone number or business contact details

## 4. How We Use Information

We use information to:
- create and secure accounts,
- verify phone ownership,
- publish and moderate reviews,
- detect fraud and abuse,
- prevent duplicate accounts and spam,
- process claims and replies,
- improve search and relevance,
- comply with legal obligations,
- maintain platform safety and trust.

## 5. Public vs Private Information

## Public information may include
- entity names
- category and location details
- review content
- ratings
- owner replies
- moderation labels such as “under verification”

## Private information may include
- phone numbers
- IP/device data
- internal abuse signals
- report metadata
- claim evidence
- moderation notes
- internal audit logs

Reviewer private account information should not be displayed publicly unless explicitly designed and consented to.

## 6. Identity and Verification

Phone verification is used to help secure the platform, reduce fake accounts, and enforce rules such as one review per entity per account.

Verification does not guarantee that every review is true, but it helps increase contribution integrity.

## 7. Content Moderation and Safety

The platform may review content using:
- automated rules,
- abuse detection systems,
- community reports,
- human moderation.

Content may be hidden, labeled, redacted, or removed when it violates platform rules.

## 8. Data Sharing

We do not sell private personal data to advertisers.

We may share data:
- with service providers that support hosting, analytics, authentication, notifications, and storage,
- where necessary to investigate abuse or security incidents,
- when required by law, court order, or lawful request,
- in connection with a business transfer or restructuring.

## 9. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary for:
- service delivery,
- fraud prevention,
- legal compliance,
- dispute handling,
- audit and moderation integrity.

Some information may be retained longer where needed for security, legal defense, or fraud prevention.

## 10. User Choices

Users may be able to:
- access their account data,
- edit limited profile information,
- delete or deactivate their account subject to platform retention rules,
- delete their own reviews where product policy allows,
- request correction of incorrect account details,
- manage cookies where applicable.

## 11. Sensitive and Prohibited Content

Users should not post:
- private phone numbers unless allowed,
- CNIC numbers,
- bank details,
- medical records,
- home addresses unless clearly part of a business/property listing policy,
- threats,
- hate speech,
- unsupported criminal accusations.

The platform may redact or remove such content.

## 12. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect information. No system is perfectly secure, and users should understand that no transmission or storage system can be guaranteed 100 percent secure.

## 13. Children

The platform is not intended for children below the applicable legal age for independent use in the target market.

## 14. Cross-Border Processing

Infrastructure or service providers may process data in jurisdictions outside the user’s location, subject to applicable safeguards.

## 15. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes should be communicated through the website, app, or other reasonable notice.

## 16. Contact

Add:
- company legal name

- postal address

## 17. Operational Notes

Because this is a review platform built around public experience-sharing, privacy design must balance:
- reviewer protection,
- entity fairness,
- abuse prevention,
- lawful compliance,
- public-interest transparency.
