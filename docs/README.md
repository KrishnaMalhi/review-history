## Core Product Thesis

This platform is not just a ratings app. It is a **trust infrastructure layer** for real-world interactions in Pakistan:
- people review entities that may not even have accounts
- trust is built through verified submissions, moderation, and community signals
- ratings alone are not enough; users need warnings, patterns, trust context, and replies
- the platform becomes more useful as more local entities are created and reviewed

## Product Principles

1. **Entity-first, not account-first**
2. **Opinion-led reviews, not accusation-led posting**
3. **High-friction trust actions, low-friction discovery**
4. **Defense-in-depth against fake reviews**
5. **Transparent moderation, not opaque censorship**
6. **Monetization must not corrupt review integrity**
7. **MVP first, enterprise-grade direction later**
